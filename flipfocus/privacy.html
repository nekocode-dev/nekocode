PRIVACY POLICY

FlipFocus by NekoCode

> LAST_UPDATED: JANUARY 2025

// INTRODUCTION

FlipFocus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices for our offline-first mobile application.

// OUR PRIVACY-FIRST APPROACH

FlipFocus is designed to be privacy-first and offline-first. We believe your learning data should stay with you, on your device.

// INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

No Data Collection:

  • No User Analytics: We do not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
  • No Study Data: Your learning progress stays on your device
  • No Content Tracking: We cannot see your flashcards or study materials
  • No Device Tracking: We do not collect device identifiers or location data

No Third-Party Tracking:

  • No Analytics Services: We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services
  • No Ad Networks: We do not integrate advertising networks that track users
  • No Social Media Tracking: We do not use social media SDKs that collect data

// INFORMATION STORED LOCALLY

On Your Device Only:

  • Flashcard Content: All your questions, answers, images, and audio stay on your device
  • Study Progress: Your learning statistics and review history remain local
  • App Settings: Preferences, themes, and customizations stored locally
  • Media Files: Any images or audio you add are stored only on your device

// CAMERA PERMISSION AND USAGE

  • Permission Requested: android.permission.CAMERA
  • Purpose: The camera is used solely for real-time scanning and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to extract text from physical materials (e.g., books, notes) that you choose to scan.
  • Processing Location: Camera frames and extracted text are processed on-device. We do not transmit camera data or OCR results to our servers.
  • Data Retention: Live camera frames are not stored. Images are only saved if you explicitly capture and save them.
  • Background Access: We do not access the camera in the background; use occurs only while you are actively in a camera/scan screen.
  • User Controls: You can deny the camera permission; the app remains usable, but scan/OCR features are disabled.

// OPTIONAL GOOGLE ACCOUNT INTEGRATION

When You Choose to Connect Google:

  • Identity Only: We only use your Google account to identify backup ownership
  • No Data Sent to Google: Your study content is not sent to Google's servers
  • Local Encryption: Backups are encrypted on your device before any storage
  • Your Control: You can disconnect Google account anytime without data loss

// DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Local Storage Only:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage
  • We use industry-standard encryption for local data protection
  • No data is transmitted to external servers during normal app usage

Backup Security (Optional):

  • When you create backups, they are encrypted on your device first
  • You control where backups are stored (local files, Google Drive via your account)
  • Backup files are encrypted and can only be restored by you

// DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We Do NOT Share Any Data:

  • Zero Data Collection: Since we don't collect data, we have nothing to share
  • No Third Parties: We do not work with data brokers, analytics companies, or advertisers
  • No Government Requests: We cannot provide data we don't have

// YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

Complete Control:

  • Full Ownership: All your data stays on your device - you own and control it completely
  • Export Anytime: Use the backup feature to export all your data
  • Delete Anytime: Uninstall the app to remove all data, or use app settings to reset
  • No Requests Needed: Since we don't collect data, you don't need to request access or deletion

// CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

FlipFocus is safe for users of all ages:

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect any personal information from anyone, including children
  • COPPA Compliant: Since we collect no data, we automatically comply with children's privacy laws
  • Parental Peace of Mind: Parents can be confident no data leaves the device

// INTERNATIONAL USERS

FlipFocus respects privacy worldwide:

  • No Data Transfers: Since data stays on your device, there are no international transfers
  • GDPR Compliant: We comply by not collecting personal data
  • Universal Privacy: Same privacy protection regardless of location

// CONTACT US

For privacy-related questions:

  • Email: nekocode.dev@gmail.com
  • In-App: Use the feedback feature for questions

Since we don't collect data, we have no privacy incidents to report and no data breaches are possible.

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to keeping your learning data private and secure on your device. Your privacy is not just important to us - it's built into our app's architecture.
