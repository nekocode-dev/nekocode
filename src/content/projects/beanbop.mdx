## Overview

BeanBop is a cute, local-first caffeine tracker designed for everyday use. It helps users log drinks quickly, monitor their daily caffeine limit, and make better choices before bedtime.

## Problem

Most tracking apps either feel heavy and clinical or require cloud accounts for basic usage. People need something simple, private, and pleasant that still gives useful caffeine guidance.

## Solution

BeanBop keeps app data on-device, focuses on fast drink logging, and provides a clear "zone" system for daily caffeine progress. It combines practical guidance with a playful UI style that users actually enjoy opening.

## Key Features

- **Offline-First Data**: Logs and preferences are stored locally.
- **Fast Add Flow**: Quickly add coffee, tea, soda, or energy drinks.
- **Daily Caffeine Zones**: Calm, Zesty, Zoomy, and Over-limit states.
- **Sleep Shield**: Estimate caffeine remaining near bedtime.
- **Import / Export Tools**: Keep data portable and under user control.
- **Privacy-Centered Design**: No cloud account required.

## Results

BeanBop is currently in active development and now includes complete legal policy pages, routing, and production-ready local data behavior ahead of launch.

## Learnings

The best wellness tools reduce friction first. Users trust and keep apps that are transparent about data handling and give immediate value in a few taps.
