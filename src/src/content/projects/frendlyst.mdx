## Overview

FrendLyst is a social discovery app built around friend value, gifts, clubs, leaderboards, and profile reputation. It turns everyday discovery and connection into a playful economy.

## Problem

Most social apps focus on passive feeds, shallow matching, or one-way attention. FrendLyst explores a more interactive model where social value, support, and community activity become visible systems.

## Solution

FrendLyst combines profile worth, market-style discovery, gifting, clubs, chat, referrals, and safety controls into one social app. Users can grow their presence, support others, and participate in community-driven progression.

## Key Features

- **Profile Worth**: Social activity, demand, and engagement contribute to a visible profile value.
- **Friend Market**: Discover people through market-style ranking and spotlight systems.
- **Gifts and Wallet**: Send gifts, earn coins, and track social economy activity.
- **Clubs**: Create communities, manage members, chat, and contribute to shared vaults.
- **Leaderboards**: Compare momentum, fame, and achievements across the network.
- **Safety Layer**: Verification, reporting, blocking, moderation, and account status flows.

## Results

FrendLyst is in active development with a production React and Capacitor app, Supabase backend, admin tools, and ongoing feature hardening.

## Learnings

Game-like social systems need strong pacing, clear rewards, and safety controls. FrendLyst balances playful market mechanics with trust, moderation, and user control.
